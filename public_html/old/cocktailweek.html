NORWICH COCKTAIL WEEK 2016.

8th - 15th October

We're really thrilled to be part of Norwich Cocktail Week again this year, it's always a great excuse to raid the liquor cabinet and try to come up with some really special drinks. As you know we at The Plasterers won the cocktail competition in both 2014 and 2015, so the winning drinks will definitely make a return to the menu. We'll also have the following to look forward to...

Home-made spirits masterclass - Wednesday 12th Oct

This will be a lesson in how spirits, infusions and liqueurs are produced and some techniques, advice and recipes for how to produce them at home. We will also make a couple of spirits/liqueurs together, so everyone will have the chance to design a drink, and will have two sample bottles to take home with them on the night.
