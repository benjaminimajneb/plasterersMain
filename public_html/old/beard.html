MOVEMBER 2015

Some of you may remember Gerald, from our Movember 2014 beer mat. We asked you to draw him a moustache, and boy, we got some goodun's.


Well to shake things up a bit, we've given you the 'tache and we'd like you to draw the face. Or maybe not a face? Who knows, it's your horrible dark imagination we're delving into. Do as you see fit.


All you need to do is find a beer mat, and draw something around the moustache. Then hand it over at the bar in The Plasterers, along with the princely sum of £1. We'll take your details, for when we need to get in touch with you because obviously your drawing will be the best, and we'll donate your £1 to the Movember charity.

If you do win the competition, we'll give you a nice case of beer, and a free pizza for you and your man/woman/dog and a big high five.

You have until the end of November (obviously).
